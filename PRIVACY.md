# Privacy Policy — Multi-Script PDF OCR

_Last updated: 13 May 2026_

## Summary
**This app collects no data.** It runs entirely offline. Nothing leaves your Mac.

## Data Collection
None. No analytics, no telemetry, no crash reporting, no user identifiers, no behavioural tracking. The app makes zero network requests in normal operation.

## Data Storage
PDF files you open are processed entirely on your Mac. File contents are never uploaded, shared, or transmitted. OCR uses Apple Vision and bundled Tesseract — both run locally.

## Network Use
The macOS `com.apple.security.network.client` entitlement is not requested. The app cannot reach the internet by design.

## Third-Party Services
None.

## Children's Privacy
The app collects no data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Permissions Used
- `com.apple.security.app-sandbox` — runs in the macOS sandbox
- `com.apple.security.files.user-selected.read-write` — reads and writes only the PDFs you explicitly open

No other entitlements are requested.
